Community Resources

Hey, we're so glad you're here!

Unfortunately, we don't have much in the way of community resources yet. BUT, here's a list of what we're working on, and what you can expect to see soon.
  • Forums: What better way for users to communicate with each other? Ask questions, find answers, share war stories.

  • User Profiles: Show off your characters, show off your achievements, show off your battlepets.
We have so many more ideas that we want to work on too, but these are the focuses right now. Let us know what you think. Don't forget to leave feedback!
